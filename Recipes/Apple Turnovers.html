Apple Turnovers


Apple turnovers using store bought puff pastry. A delicious breakfast or easy dessert.

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • Puff Pastry
  • AP Flour (all purpose)
  • Granny Smith Apples
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Heavy Cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Remove puff pastry from freezer and thaw according to package directions
  3. Peel and dice apples.
  4. In a mediumn pot, melt 1 tablespoon butter over medium heat. Add diced apples and cook until apples are softened.
  5. Reduce heat. Stir in 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, and salt. Continue to simmer for 3 minutes until apples are soft and caramelized. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  6. Using a rolling pin, roll the first sheet of thawed puff pastry to an 11" square. With a knife, cut into 4 equal sized squares.
  8. Place cooled apple mixture over half of each square, leaving at least a 1/2" border.
  9. Thoroughly beat together 1 egg and 1 tbsn water. Brush eggwash lightly on edges of the pastry. Bring the edges together and seal. Crimp together with back of fork tines. Repeat steps with the 2nd sheet of puff pastry to make 8 turnovers.
